Stok Kangri

Located in Ladakh, this is one of the most fascinating places in this earth. In the past few years, this peak has gained immense popularity. While illustrating the entire Himalayan range with a wider perspective, this trek embraces the deserted surroundings while offering the trekkers the scope to experience total peace. Besides, this track also includes different ridges and therefore the tourists can experience the sudden change in scenery. So, from this peak, the travelers can experience the total view of the Indus Valley and Zanskar.

Detailed Itinerary

Day-01:

Arrive at Leh: This is the first step of trekking to Stok Kangri. Leh can be reached easily from Delhi. Travelers can first spend one day in Leh to become accustomed to the weather while enjoying the natural and cultural beauty of Leh. Besides, there are a number of ancient temples and monasteries, which they can explore.

Day-02:

Leh (11500 ft.) to Chang Ma (13087 ft.) via Stok Village (11800 ft.): This day begins with an adventurous drive to Stok Village from Leh while enjoying different beautiful and exotic scenes. Therefore, the track continues to Chang Ma. Sometimes, this trek is little difficult.

Day-03:

Chang Ma (13087 ft.) to Mankorma (14200 ft.): Next day, the trek starts from Chang Ma and continues to Mankorma. Here the entire track passes through barren grey landscape of Ladakh. Or it can be said that this trek will show the real beauty of Ladakh.

Day-04:

Mankorma (14200 ft.) to Base camp (16300 ft.): This is a few hours trek from Mankorma and the experience is really pleasurable. The place includes several water bodies in the form of water streams and lakes. Besides, there are a number of peaks here like as the Parcha Kangri, Gulap Kangri, and several others.

Day-05:

Rest day + training day: It is a simple day for the trekkers as they get the time to go around and learn about the place in a much better way. Basically, the travelers are trained on this day for the upcoming trek and its challenges and possibilities included.

Day-06:

Base camp (16300 ft.) to Stok Kangri (20080 ft.) back to base camp: Next day, the trek to Stok Kangri will be started. The route includes a few glaciers and steep slopes until the summit ridge. The entire route offers an amazing view of the area

Day-07:

Base camp to Leh: It is the ultimate day of the trekking experience. On this day, the tourists will experience an amazing view of the mountain ranges and snow clad beauty while returning to Leh from the base camp.

Departure from Leh: It is the last phase of Stok Kangri trek. Here the tourists are sent back to Delhi.
