Lamayuru to Darcha Trek

It is one of the most popular treks in Ladakh that connects Himachal (Darcha), Zanskar (Padum) and Ladakh (Leh). This trek is entirely different and includes amazing experience of the intact land of Lamayuru ans zanskar regions. Besides, this trek also focuses on the monks, monasteries, villages and their people.

Trek itinerary:

Day-01:

Leh – Photoksar via Lamayuru (5 hrs drive): The trip starts with a jeep ride from Leh to Photoksar via Lamayuru. Here the travelers will come across some of the most famous and oldest monasteries in Ladakh. The road then continues to the wide Spong Valley and therefore the gradual and long ascent to Sir Sir La starts. From here, the travelers can enjoy wonderful views of the main Zanskar range and of the Singge la (5090m). The next destination is the village of Photoksar, situated among the mountain cliffs and enclosed in the green fields.

Day-02:

Photoksar – Base of Singe la (5- 6 hrs): Next day, the travelers first reach to the lush and broad Photang valley after crossing the small Bumiktse la pass. Therefore, the track ascends towards the camp at the Singe la base.

Day-03:

Base of Singe la – Gongma via Singe la (6-7 hrs): The next day starts at the Singe la base. Though this place is steep, but the top area of the pass presents a really amazing view. Therefore, the track goes down in a steep zigag format to the village of Gongma.

Day-04:

Gongma- Lanang via Lingshed (6- 7 hrs): From the destination of Gongma, the track converts into a gentle trail to the Margum La. Lingshed is just an hour from this place. There is a monastery founded by the Gelugpa in Lingshed, which tourists can enjoy. Therefore, they can continue towards Lanang.

Day-05:

Lanang- Zingchen via Hanumur la (6-7 hrs): Next day, the trek starts to the pass of Hanumur la. From this pass, the views to Singe la and other snowcapped peaks of the Ladakh range are mesmerizing. There is also a sheer descend down to Snertse. After resting to this place for some time, tourists will continue towards Zingchen and can take bath in the Oma Chhu River.

Day-06:

Zingchen- Hanamur via Parfi la (4- 5 hrs): This day starts with crossing the last pass before Padum, Parfi la. The track descends down to the banks of the Zanskar River and therefore to the village of Hanamur.

Day-07:

Hanamur- Pishu (4-5 hrs): On this day, the trek starts from Hanamur along the Zanskar River. The journey to Pishu is of 2-3 hours. Together Pidmo, Pishu and Hanamur along with Honia and Tsazar formed the former kingdom of Zangla.

Day-08:

Pishu – Padum (6-7 hrs): The track leads to Rinam from Pishu, following the Zanskar River. Therefore, the trek continues to Padum.

Day-09:

Padum – Raru (6 hrs): Until Raru, the traveler will have to follow one dusty jeep road while passing by the villages of Shila and Bardan. From Bardhan, this will take several more hours before entering a plateau and the monastery and village of Mune, which is another 40 min walk to the camp.

Day-10:

Raru- Pepula (5-6 hrs): The track follows a flat ground from Raru before steeply descending to the Tsarap River. The track then continues to Pepula.

Day-11:

Pepula- Purne (5-6 hrs): The trail is quite tricky from Pepula with loads of ups and downs and small side rivers towards the villages of Surle and Char and then the track will climb up to Purne.

Day-12:

Dayhike to Phuktal Monastery 3850m (4 hrs): The trek this day continues to Phuktal monastery, the most spectacular situated gompa in Ladakh. After visiting the monastery, tourists will return back to Purne.

Day-13:

Purne - Tetha (2 hrs): On the next day, the journey starts from purne and finishes at the fertile, big plateau of the whitewashed village of Tetha.

Day-14:

Tetha - Kargyak (5 hrs): This day, it is a gentle walk from Tetha to Katgyak while crossing the villages like Karu and Tanze.

Day-15:

Kargyak - Lakhang (4- 5 hrs): On this day, the tourists will reach to the shepherds encampment of Lakang (4470m). Here the tourists will enjoy a panoramic and amazing view of the entire Himalaya Range.

Day-16:

Lakhang - Ramjak via Shinkul la (7-8 hrs): The day begins with a steep climb before the trail crosses one alpine tract towards the Shinkul la base following a track of snowfields or scree. Therefore, the travelers will reach to Ramjak camp.

Day-17:

Ramjak- Zanskar Sumdo (5-6 hrs) and drive to Keylong: This day, the track leads through a few and meager grazing pastures and boulder fields to Palamo. Therefore, the tourists will enjoy a drive to Keylong.

Day-18:

Drive to Manali ( 4-5 hours): The ending day of the trek starts with a drive to Manali.
