Green Lake Trek

Green Lake in North Sikkim is known as one of the most captivating treks located in the base camp of Mount Kanchenjunga. It is a wonderful trek to find different types of blue poppies, Rhododendrons and Primulas in a great bloom. The trek brings out the world experience of the mountainous view along the peaks with Kanchenjunga at the head of the Green Lake Valley.

As the Green Lake Base Camp is stretched for 5000 meters, therefore the trek is graded from moderate to strenuous and in some cases demanding. This trek will take the trekkers through river valleys, hills and villages along the mounts of the great Kanchenjunga Ranges with some pristine areas. This thing makes this trek just ideal for both flower lovers and bird watchers.

Detailed itinerary:

Day-01:

Bagdogra/NJP railway station- Gangtok: Travelers first need to reach to Bagdogra/NJP railway station and from there they can drive to Gangtok. Therefore, they can stay in any hotels.

Day-02:

Gangtok to Lachen: The next day starts with a drive to Lachen, located in North Sikkim. Travelers can stay that night at any hotels in Lachen. On the next day, they can explore the sights in and around Lachen and therefore can stay that night at Lachen.

Day-03:

Lachen to Tallem: This day begins with a walk towards Zema, the confluence of Zemu Chu and Thangu Chu. After crossing a bridge, the track turns towards West following the left bank of Zemu Chu to the Green Lake. For the initial 6 kms, the track is broad and wide. Therefore, it comes Tallem, located at the confluence of Zemu Chu and Lhonak Chu. This is the first base camp of this trek.

Day-04:

Tallem to Jakthang: 2nd day, the track start with entering to some great areas for which Sikkim is well known. Travelers need to cross one bridge to steadily climb through the forest to Shobuk. Then the trail steeply descends for 12 kms.

Day-05:

Jakthang to Yabuk: On this day too, the track continues through forest. The travelers need to cross a bridge over the Thombak Chu after one short climb. There are quite a few descends and ascends on this route. After 8 kms of walk, tourists reach to Yabuk, a beautiful yet small camping place near the glacier.

Day-06:

Yabuk to rest camp: This day, the track enters to the moraine field of Zemu Glacier from Yabuk. This is a tiring route with enhancing altitude and the latter part is rewarded with amazing views of Simvu and Siniolchu mountain peaks. Besides, one can also view Kangchenjunga rising in the distance. The next rest camp is at the mouth of Lhonak valley.

Day-07:

Rest camp to Green Lake: Next day, the track starts from the Rest Camp to the Green Lake along moraine ridge. During this trail, the views of surrounding peaks are really clear. The final destination is Green Lake camp, an open vast ground. The next day is the rest day. Tourists can roam around the green lake.

Day-08:

Green Lake to Yabuk: This day marks the beginning of return journey. First day, the travelers reach to the rest camp in Yabuk.

Day-09:

Yabuk to Tallem: On this day, the tourists trek back to Tallem from Yabuk.

Day-010:

Tallem to Lachen: The next day, the travelers start descending towards Jakthang from Tallem.

Day-011:

Lachen to Gangtok: This day, the tourists are driven back to Gangtok.

Day-012:

Gangtok to Bagdogra: Tourists are sent to Bagdogra airport/NJP Railway Station in the morning. From there, they can board onward train/flight.
